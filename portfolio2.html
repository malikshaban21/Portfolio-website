Mobile App for Task Management

This project involved designing a feature-rich task management mobile application aimed at helping users organize their daily routines and long-term projects. The app allows for task prioritization, deadline management, and real-time synchronization across devices. Its clean, minimalistic design ensures ease of use, while advanced features like notifications and progress tracking keep users on track with their goals.

  • Features:
  • Customizable Categories: Helps users organize their tasks by type or priority..
  • Push Notifications: Alerts for upcoming deadlines or overdue tasks.
  • Data Syncing: Cloud-based syncing ensures data is consistent across devices.
  • Progress Tracking: Visual indicators like progress bars motivate productivity.
